Food supplements

To be healthy and active, our body needs more than 100 different substances every day. Almost 40% of them the body is unable to synthesize (produce) by itself and must obtain from food.

What are Food Supplements?

Dietary supplements are products designed to supplement the diet by providing essential nutrients, which may be lacking in one's regular meals. They can include vitamins, minerals, amino acids, botanicals, enzymes, and various other substances

Buy now

Why Consider Food Supplements?

Nutritional Gaps: Many people struggle to get all the essential nutrients they need from their diet alone. Supplements can fill these gaps, ensuring the body receives the necessary vitamins and minerals.

Specific Health Goals: Some individuals use supplements to support particular health goals, such as improving bone density, boosting the immune system, enhancing sports performance, or promoting hair and skin health.

Common Types of Food Supplements

Vitamins: These micronutrients are essential for various bodily functions. Common vitamin supplements include Vitamin C, Vitamin D, and B-complex vitamins.

Minerals: Essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and iron play critical roles in maintaining health and well-being.

Herbal and Botanical Supplements

These include products like ginseng, turmeric, and echinacea, which have been used for centuries in traditional medicine.

Protein Supplements: Popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts, protein powders can aid in muscle recovery and growth.
